About the Guild

What is Side Story Club?

✦

Small detours during a long journey often become the most memorable moments shared between party members. Side Story Club creates a space where people can pause their main quest and enjoy simple memories together.

Inspired by old JRPG guidebooks, quest menus, and hand-drawn fantasy illustrations — we print everything in black and white through thermal print, creating nostalgic and timeless photostrips.
